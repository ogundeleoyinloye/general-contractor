About Us

Phoenix Home Remodeling was started because the industry didn’t give homeowners the control they deserve. Our unique & innovative design-build approach gives you unparalleled control and insight into the design and construction. Our design-build process also allows you to ‘test drive’ us without committing to the construction agreement. So you can plan your whole remodel, and know what you’re getting before signing your construction agreement. You also get a fixed price because every aspect of your remodel was thoroughly planned beforehand. So there are no hidden fees! As a homeowner, using a design-build company is the one way you can protect yourself from many of the contractor nightmares.
